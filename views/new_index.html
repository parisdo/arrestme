Our goal is to help law enforcement officers efficiently patrol areas that are unsafe. Police officers are always keeping our best interests in mind, so we created a web-app to help these officers visualize their crime data. We have taken data from DELPHI and represented it in a very visual fashion; we have our crime-density map on the right side, as well as a graph showing the number of crimes logged per agency. Click the button below and let's get started by looking at the top crimes per community!
